Project Details: HR Analytics Dashboard for Employee Insights

Project Overview:

The HR Analytics Dashboard is a Tableau-based data visualization project that provides comprehensive insights into various HR metrics and workforce trends within our organization. This interactive dashboard showcases key HR metrics, including employee count, iteration count, active employees, department-wise iteration, number of employees by age group, education-wise attrition, job satisfaction rating, attrition by gender, and attrition rate by gender for different age groups. The visualization enables our HR team to make data-driven decisions related to employee retention, performance management, and talent development.

Key Visualizations:

  1. Employee Count and Iteration Count:

    The dashboard displays the total count of employees and the number of times employees have been through the iteration process. Visualizations over time help identify trends in workforce growth and development.

  2. Active Employees:

    An interactive chart illustrates the number of active employees currently working in the organization. Changes in active employees over time are highlighted for quick reference.

  3. Department-wise Iteration:

    The dashboard provides a breakdown of the iteration count by department. A treemap visualization allows for a clear understanding of the relative iteration count for each department.

  4. Number of Employees by Age Group:

    Employees are categorized into age groups (e.g., 20-30, 31-40, 41-50, 50+). A dynamic pie chart showcases the distribution of employees across different age groups.

  5. Education-wise Attrition:

    Attrition rates are compared based on employees' educational qualifications. A stacked bar chart visualizes attrition trends for each education category.

  6. Job Satisfaction Rating:

    The dashboard presents the average job satisfaction rating based on employee surveys. Any significant changes in satisfaction ratings over time are easily discernible.

  7. Attrition by Gender:

    An interactive donut chart illustrates the proportion of attrition for each gender.

  8. Attrition Rate by Gender for Different Age Groups:

    Attrition rates for each gender across various age groups are displayed through interactive line charts.

Conclusion:

The HR Analytics Dashboard project has been successfully completed, providing valuable insights into our workforce data. The interactive visualizations allow HR managers and executives to explore the data and gain actionable insights dynamically. With this tool, our HR team can now make informed decisions to enhance employee satisfaction, reduce attrition, and improve overall workforce management. The project showcases our proficiency in data visualization using Tableau and our ability to derive meaningful insights from raw HR data.
