Project details

The Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard aims to provide comprehensive insights into the emergency room's patient demographics, satisfaction, referral patterns, and busy hours. Utilizing Tableau, this interactive and visually appealing dashboard will enable hospital administrators and staff to make data-driven decisions, optimize resources, and enhance patient experiences in the emergency room.

Dashboard Components:

1. ER Visits Overview: - Total number of patients visiting the ER (overall and filtered by time period). - Trend chart depicting the variation in ER visits over time.
2. Gender Breakdown:
- Pie chart showing the distribution of patients visiting the ER by gender.
- Interactive filter for selecting a specific gender to view related statistics.
3. Average Patient Satisfaction Score:
- Gauge chart displaying the average patient satisfaction score in real-time.
- Line chart showcasing the trend in patient satisfaction over time.
4. Referrals by Department:
- Horizontal bar chart illustrating the number of referrals made by various departments.
- Drop-down filter to select a specific department and view associated details.
5. Distribution of Patients by Race:
- Stacked bar chart presenting the racial distribution of patients in the ER.
- Drill-down capability to view race distribution by gender or age group.
6. Distribution of Patients by Age:
- Histogram or grouped bar chart visualizing the age-wise distribution of ER patients.
- Ability to interactively modify age group intervals.
7. Busy Hours Heat Map:
- Heat map representing the busiest hours in the emergency room throughout the week.
- A color-coded scale depicting the intensity of patient visits.

Data Sources:


The data for this dashboard will be derived from the hospital's electronic health record (EHR) system, patient satisfaction surveys, and referral records. The datasets will be integrated and transformed into a clean, structured format suitable for Tableau visualization.

Technical Approach:


1. Data Collection: Extract and gather relevant data from the hospital's EHR system, satisfaction surveys, and referral records.
2. Data Preparation: Cleanse, transform, and preprocess the data to ensure accuracy and consistency.
3. Data Modeling: Create necessary calculated fields or aggregates to derive meaningful insights.
4. Dashboard Design: Design an aesthetically pleasing and intuitive dashboard layout with appropriate color schemes and visualizations.
5. Dashboard Development: Utilize Tableau's features to build interactive filters, parameters, and actions for dynamic user experience.
6. Data Integration: Connect the prepared datasets to Tableau for real-time updates.
7. Testing and Feedback: Thoroughly test the dashboard's functionality and accuracy, gather feedback from stakeholders, and make necessary improvements.
8. Deployment and Training: Deploy the finalized dashboard to a secure platform accessible by authorized personnel. Conduct training sessions to familiarize the users with the dashboard's functionalities.

Project Outcome:

The Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard will empower hospital administrators, physicians, and staff with valuable insights into patient demographics, satisfaction, referral patterns, and busy hours. By leveraging this data-driven approach, the hospital can optimize resources, enhance patient care, and streamline operations in the emergency room, ultimately leading to improved patient experiences and outcomes.
